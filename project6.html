Yes! Dumbo Improvement District

CREATIVE DIRECTION:

Stefan Sagmeister

ILLUSTRATION:

Yoko Shimizu

MURAL PAINTER:

Coby Kennedy

DESIGN:

Wade Jeffree

The studio was commissioned by the DUMBO Improvement District in partnership with Two Trees Management Co and the NYCDOT Urban Art Program to paint two 80 foot long murals on the walls of the Brooklyn-Queens Expressway underpass, on Jay Street in Dumbo. We collaborated with renowned Japanese illustrator Yuko Shimizu and created two large typographic messages to sit on the facing walls.
