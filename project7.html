Take It On SVA Posters

CREATIVE DIRECTION:

Stefan Sagmeister

DESIGN:

Stefan Sagmeister, Jessica Walsh and Santiago Carrasquilla

PHOTOGRAPHY:

Henry Leutwyler

RETOUCHING:

Erik Johansson

"Take it On" posters for the School of Visual Arts (SVA) in New York City. We looked around our studio and realized that the Sagmeister and Walsh team is rooted in the SVA community. Stefan teaches at the MFA Design department, Jessica teaches at the BFA Design department, and Santiago was a student at the BFA Design department. We embraced the maxim by literally taking on the typography on our faces. We worked with renowned photographer Henry Leutwyler and creative retoucher Erik Johansson to achieve this poster series which is now displayed throughout the NYC subway stations.

Sagmeister and Walsh, 2013

Disciplines: Advertising, Print
