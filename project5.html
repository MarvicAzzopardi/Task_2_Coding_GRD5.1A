Over the course of the last century, beauty was displaced by functionality in design and architecture. As a result, something essential was lost. Beuaty not only impacts the way we feel, it changes the way we behave. Sagmeister and Walsh show us how this shortsighted disregard of beauty can be reversed.

Sagmeister and Walsh, 2018
